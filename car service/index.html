WHY SERVICE YOUR CAR REGULARLY?
Regular servicing of your car is an important part of being an owner of car. We depend so much on travelling from one place to another. When the unwanted things happen and if car breaks down, it often ruins that time. Servicing a car regularly does not assure certain failure but it will certainly help to reduce the intensity of loss. It is always recommended that an engine oil should be completely drained while doing servicing. The oil must be inspected for any impurities or metal fragments which could indicate engine wear. Engine Oil is like a blood of engine. You must always try to ensure that engine oil condition is not too much thick.

1. Changing oil ,flat tire, spark plugs, 2. Removing scratches from paint, 3. Replacing air filters.
4. Changing a car battery, 5.Replacing a headlight or taillight ,6. Replacing wipers.
